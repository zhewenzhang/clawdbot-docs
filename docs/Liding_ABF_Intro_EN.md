# Liding Semiconductor - ABF Advanced Packaging Substrate

## Company Overview
Liding Semiconductor specializes in high-end ABF (Ajinomoto Build-up Film) substrates for advanced IC packaging.

## Product Lines

### 1. Server CPU Packaging
- Supports 7-11 chiplets, even 18-chip integration
- 200,000+ bumps (up from 100,000)
- 20-26 layer mass production
- Coming: 28-30+ layers

### 2. AI Chip Packaging
- Compatible with HBM configurations: 4+2, 8+4, 12+ units
- Low dielectric constant for high-speed signals
- Thermal stability for 700-1000W power chips
- Partners with leading global chip companies

### 3. Auto & AI Interconnect Chips
- Compact "PCB-Substrate-Chip" integration
- High reliability for automotive environments
- Supports autonomous driving & IoT applications
